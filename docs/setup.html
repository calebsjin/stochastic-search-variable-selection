Stochastic Search Variable Selection (SSVS)

Chapter 2 Setup

Let \({\bf D}=({\bf M},{\bf y})\) be a dataset, where \({\bf M}\in \mathbb{R}^{n\times p}\) and \({\bf y}\in \mathbb{R}^{n}\) with \(n=100\) and \(p=500\). We are interested in finding predictors related to the response. Consider a high dimensional linear regression model as follows: \[\begin{eqnarray} {\bf y}={\bf X}{\boldsymbol \beta}+ {\boldsymbol \epsilon} \tag{2.1} \end{eqnarray}\] where \({\bf y}=(y_1,y_2,\ldots,y_{n})^{{\top}}\) is the \(n\)-dimensional response vector, \({\bf X}=[1,{\bf M}]=[{\bf x}_0, {\bf x}_1,\ldots,{\bf x}_{p}]\) is the \(n\times (p+1)\) design matrix, and \({\boldsymbol \epsilon}\sim \mathcal{N}_{n}({\bf 0},\sigma^2{\bf I}_{n})\). Note that as \(p+1>n\), \({\bf X}\) is not full rank.
